Inter-County Train: Departs both Mombasa and Nairobi Terminus simultaneously at 8:00 AM every day. This service entails scheduled stops at Mariakani, Miasenyi, Voi, Mtito Andei, Kibwezi, Emali and Athi River stations.

Afternoon Express Train: Departs both Mombasa and Nairobi Terminus simultaneously at 3:00 PM and includes a stop over at Voi Station.

Night Train: Departs both Mombasa and Nairobi Terminus simultaneously at 10:00 PM. This services does not entail stops during the journey.
